# 👨‍✈️ Airline Pulse — Pilot Guide

> Your complete guide to the Airline Pulse dashboard. Everything you need to know as a pilot.

---

## 🔑 How to Access

1. Log in to the virtual airline website
2. Click **"Airline Pulse"** in the navigation menu (or go to `/airline-info-pulse`)
3. The dashboard loads automatically with your data

> You must be logged in. The page is not visible to guests.

---

## 🌐 Language

The dashboard supports **German** and **English**. To switch:

1. Click the **flag icon** (🇩🇪 / 🇬🇧) in the top navigation bar of your VA website
2. The page will reload in the selected language

---

## ⏰ Time Filter

At the top of the page, you'll find filter buttons:

| Button | Period |
|--------|--------|
| **Today** | Only today's data |
| **Yesterday** | Yesterday's data |
| **Week** | Current week (Monday – Sunday) |
| **Month** | Current calendar month |
| **Quarter** | Current quarter (3 months) |
| **Year** | Current calendar year |

Clicking a filter updates all sections that are time-dependent (KPIs, Top Lists, Feed, Snapshot). Some sections like Missions always show lifetime data.

## 📊 The Dashboard Sections

### KPI Strip (Top Bar)

The colored cards at the top show **all pilots' combined stats** for the selected time period:

| Card | What it shows |
|------|--------------|
| ✈️ **Flights** | Total number of accepted flights by all pilots |
| ⏱️ **Block Time** | Total flight hours (block time) of all pilots |
| 📏 **Distance** | Total nautical miles flown by all pilots |
| ⛽ **Fuel** | Total fuel consumed (kg) by all pilots |
| 📉 **Avg Landing Rate** | Average landing rate across all flights (fpm) |
| ✅ **Accepted PIREPs** | Total accepted pilot reports |

The **colored arrows** (↑ / ↓ with percentage) show the change compared to the previous equivalent period. For example, if you're looking at "This Month", the arrow compares to last month.

---

### Cockpit (Your Personal Stats)

This section shows **your data only**:

- **Your Name** — With your most-flown airline and aircraft
- **Progress Bar** — How far you are toward the monthly flight hour goal
- **Remaining Time** — How many hours you still need this month
- **Your 6 KPI Tiles** — Flights, block time, distance, fuel, avg landing, and accepted PIREPs for the selected period

> Only you can see your cockpit. Other pilots see their own data here.

---

### Missions

Missions show your **lifetime achievements** (not limited by the time filter):

| Mission | What it tracks |
|---------|---------------|
| 🔥 **Flight Streak** | Consecutive days with at least 1 flight. Keep flying daily to grow your streak! |
| 🏅 **Next Milestone** | Progress toward the next flight count milestone (10, 25, 50, 100, 200, 500, …). The progress bar shows how close you are. |
| 🏆 **Your Ranking** | Your position among all pilots, sorted by total flights. If you're in the Top 3, you'll see a podium badge! |
| 🗺️ **Airport Explorer** | Number of unique airports you've departed from or arrived at. |
| ✈️ **Fleet Types** | How many different aircraft types (subfleets) you've flown. |
| 📉 **Personal Record** | Your best (softest) landing rate ever in fpm. Lower absolute value = better! |
| ⚡ **Daily Challenge** | How many flights you've completed today vs. the daily goal (default: 3). |
| 📏 **Longest Flight** | Your longest single flight by distance (NM) with the route displayed. |
| 🏢 **Airlines Flown** | How many of the VA's airlines you've flown for. Try to complete the collection! |
| 🌅 **Weekend Pilot** | Percentage of your flights that were on Saturday or Sunday. |

### Pilot Duel

Want to compare yourself to another pilot?

1. Find the **"Compare with:"** dropdown next to the Missions section
2. Select a pilot from the list
3. A comparison table appears below, showing your stats side-by-side

**How scoring works:**

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🟢 (green highlight) | You win this category |
| 🟠 (amber highlight) | Your rival wins |
| ⚪ (no highlight) | Tie |

The score is displayed as "X : Y" (your wins vs. rival's wins).

**Categories compared:** Flights, block time, streak, milestone, ranking, airports, fleet types, best landing, today's flights, longest flight, airlines flown, weekend percentage.

### Quick Start

Shows **random flight suggestions** from the flight schedule to give you inspiration for your next flight. Use the region buttons to filter:

| Region | Airports starting with |
|--------|----------------------|
| 🇪🇺 **Europe** | E (EGLL, EDDF, …), L (LFPG, LIRF, …), U (UUEE, …) |
| 🌏 **Asia** | Z (ZBAA, …), V (VHHH, …), R (RJTT, …), W, O |
| 🇺🇸 **USA** | K (KJFK, KLAX, …) |

Each card shows the route (departure → arrival), airline, distance, flight time, and available aircraft types.

### Top Pilots

The **Top 10** (or more) pilots ranked by your chosen criterion:

| Sort Option | What it ranks by |
|------------|-----------------|
| **by Flights** | Number of accepted flights |
| **by Block Time** | Total flight hours |
| **by Distance** | Total nautical miles |

Each pilot entry shows:
- 🏅 Rank medal (🥇🥈🥉 for top 3)
- Flight count and block time
- Landing rate (if available)
- Top routes (most frequently flown)
- Expandable "Recent Flights" with individual landing rates

Click **"Show more"** to see all pilots beyond the top 10.

---

### Top Aircraft

Similar to Top Pilots, but for aircraft:

- Ranked by flights or block time
- Shows registration, type, airline
- Fuel efficiency in **kg/NM** (fuel per nautical mile)
- CO₂ emissions in tonnes
- Top routes and recent flights (expandable)

---

### Activity Feed

A chronological feed of everything happening in the VA:

| Event Type | Icon | Shows |
|------------|------|-------|
| ✈️ **Flight completed** | Blue plane | Pilot, route, airline, aircraft, flight time, landing rate |
| 👤 **New pilot** | Green person | Name with link to profile |
| 🔧 **Maintenance** | Wrench (color-coded) | Check type, aircraft registration and type |

Landing rates are color-coded:

| Color | Range | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 🟢 Green | 0 to -299 fpm | Butter landing! |
| 🟡 Orange | -300 to -499 fpm | Acceptable |
| 🔴 Red | -500 fpm or worse | Hard landing |

---

### Airline Snapshot

Overview of all airlines in the VA:

- **Top 4** shown as large cards with:
  - Flight count and share percentage
  - Block time and distance
  - Average landing rate
  - Pilot count
  - Trend arrow compared to previous period
- **Remaining airlines** shown in a compact list with the same data

## 💡 Tips to Improve Your Stats

| Goal | How to achieve it |
|------|------------------|
| **Grow your streak** | Fly at least 1 short flight every day |
| **Improve landing rate** | Practice approaches in your sim, use ILS |
| **Climb the ranking** | Consistent daily flights beat occasional long flights |
| **Explore airports** | Try new destinations you haven't visited |
| **Complete airlines** | Fly at least once for each airline in the VA |
| **Weekend bonus** | Saturday and Sunday flights count toward the Weekend Pilot mission |
| **Hit milestones faster** | Short-haul flights count the same as long-haul for the milestone counter |

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Why does my cockpit show 0 flights even though I flew today?**  
A: The PIREP must be **accepted** by the system (or admin). Pending or rejected PIREPs are not counted.

**Q: Can other pilots see my personal stats?**  
A: Your Cockpit section is private. However, your flights are visible in Top Pilots, the Activity Feed, and if someone selects you in the Pilot Duel.

**Q: What is "block time"?**  
A: Block time is the total time from gate to gate (pushback to parking), as reported in your PIREP. It's the standard aviation metric for flight duration.

**Q: Why is my landing rate different from what my ACARS showed?**  
A: The dashboard shows the landing rate as recorded in the accepted PIREP. Some ACARS clients may report slightly different values due to timing or measurement method.

**Q: How is the monthly goal calculated?**  
A: The default goal is 4 hours (240 minutes) of block time per month. Your VA admin can change this in the module configuration.

**Q: Can I see data from previous months/years?**  
A: Use the time filter buttons (Week, Month, Quarter, Year) to view different periods. Missions always show your lifetime totals.

**Q: What is the CO₂ value on aircraft?**  
A: It's calculated as fuel consumed × 3.16 (the standard carbon emission factor for jet fuel). It's a rough estimate for fun, not a scientific measurement.
